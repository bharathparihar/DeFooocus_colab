# Buyer Features Guide

This document defines what a **Buyer** (Customer) can see and do on the website.

## Overview
The Buyer Catalog is the public-facing side of the shop. It is designed to be a mobile-first experience, allowing customers to easily browse products, services, and contact the seller.

## 1. Essentials & Navigation
- **Welcome Popup**: Interactive full-screen greeting with custom images/messages on first visit.
- **Announcement Bar**: High-visibility scrolling updates fixed at the top of the storefront.
- **Banner & Logo**: Dynamic branding area supporting high-quality portraits, landscapes, and **video banners**.
- **Floating Bottom Bar**: Glassmorphism-styled persistent actions for **Quick Call**, **WhatsApp Chat**, **Interactive Maps**, and **Profile Sharing**.
- **Navigation Menu**: Smooth slide-in menu for rapid access to **Shop**, **Gallery**, **About Us**, and **Business Hours**.

## 2. Shop Content
- **Shop Stats**: Real-time trust signals (Verified Client counts, Portfolio size, and Star Ratings).
- **Categories**: Elegantly designed, horizontally scrollable chips to filter the catalog.
- **Featured Film**: Full-width cinematic brand story or product showcase (YouTube integration).
- **Gallery**: Immersive 1:1 square grid view support for both professional photography and **short-form videos**.
- **Social Embeds**: Live interactive feeds from Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube to show social proof.
- **Business Hours**: Micro-interaction shows current "Open/Closed" status with a detailed weekly schedule.
- **Visit Us**: Precise physical address with a "One-Click Navigate" button and seamless Google Maps integration.
- **Search & Filter**: Instant, character-by-character search bar for finding specific products or services.

## 3. Products & Services
- **Products Catalog**: Comprehensive storefront in a **Premium 2-column Mobile Grid**. Supports auto-playing video 1:1 cards.
- **Services List**: Professionally formatted grid detailing specialized offerings with rich descriptions.
- **WhatsApp Commerce**: "Order Now" triggers a pre-filled cart message directly to the seller's WhatsApp.
- **Catalog PDF**: Instant request button to receive a detailed PDF brochure or price list via chat.

## 4. Interaction & Engagement
- **Inquiry Form**: Direct lead generation form for private questions, automatically tracked in the seller's dashboard.
- **Appointment Booking**: Integrated calendar-style request form for scheduling services and consultations.
- **Testimonials**: Dynamic slider showing authentic customer feedback with rating aggregation.
- **Action Buttons**: Instant-trigger buttons for **Email**, **Direct Call**, or **WhatsApp Support**.
- **Share Profile**: One-tap QR code generator for networking and "Copy URL" functionality with full SEO card support.

## 5. Information & Connect
- **FAQ**: Browse frequently asked questions.
- **Social Links**: Icons for connecting on other platforms (Instagram, etc.).
- **Seller Login**: Footer link for business owners to manage the shop.

## 6. Technical Features
- **PWA Install**: Fully compliant "Single Alias App" - one tap install to Android or iOS home screens.
- **SEO Optimized**: Custom Meta Titles, descriptions, and **Structured Data** for max Google visibility.
- **Social Cards**: Custom share images automatically appear when the link is shared on WhatsApp or Social Media.
- **Performance**: High-speed loading with optimized media delivery and global CDN support.
- **Custom Branding**: Supports seller-defined CSS themes and custom JS for an exclusive brand feel.
